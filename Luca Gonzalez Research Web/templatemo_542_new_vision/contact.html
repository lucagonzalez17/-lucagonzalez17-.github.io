Additional Information

Big Data is the name given by statisticians to amounts of data on such a level of volume and complexity that cannot be handled by conventional software.Inside this science Big Data focuses on the capture and processing of data while Analytics is the part that examines the information trying to optimize decisions.According to experts ,Big Data has five parameters that determines it: Velocity ,Volume ,Variety ,Value and Veracity.(Sakyi,2016)

More Additional Information

Although the size used to determine whether a given data set is considered big data is not strictly defined and will continue to change year by year, today most analysts and professionals use the 30-50 TB range to multiple data sets PB-level data. Nowadays most of the data generated by technology is unstructured so this makes Big Data very complex.
